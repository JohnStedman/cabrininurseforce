## 1. Introduction

### What is NurseForce?

NurseForce is a comprehensive workforce management platform designed specifically for healthcare organizations managing casual, agency, and supplementary nursing staff at short notice. Built for Cabrini Health and powered by Torrens Health, the platform enables efficient management of 69,000+ shifts annually with:

- **Rapid Shift Allocation** – Fill urgent shifts in seconds
- **Real-Time Visibility** – Live workforce tracking across all locations
- **Roster Integration** – Seamless coordination with existing systems
- **Unified Workforce Management** – Manage hospital, casual, and agency staff in one place
- **Live Compliance Tracking** – Monitor AHPRA registrations and credentials
- **Complete Audit Trails** – Full transparency and accountability
- **Advanced Analytics** – Data-driven workforce insights

### Who Should Use This Manual?

This manual is designed for:
- **Workforce Coordinators** – Managing day-to-day shift allocations
- **Department Managers** – Overseeing staffing for specific wards/departments
- **Finance Teams** – Monitoring workforce budgets and agency spend
- **Compliance Officers** – Tracking credentials and registrations
- **System Administrators** – Configuring and maintaining the platform

1. Navigate to the NurseForce login page

3. **Enable Notifications** – Set up alerts for urgent shifts and compliance issues

## 7. Smart Match (AI-Powered)

NurseForce's AI engine automatically matches staff to shifts based on multiple factors.

### How Smart Match Works

The AI analyzes:
1. **Skills & Qualifications** – Exact role requirements
2. **Compliance Status** – AHPRA registration, credentials
3. **Availability** – Current schedule and preferences
4. **Location** – Distance from facility
5. **Performance History** – Past shift ratings
6. **Cost** – Optimize for budget efficiency
7. **Fairness** – Distribute shifts equitably

### Using Smart Match

**For Single Shift:**
1. Navigate to unfilled shift
2. Click **Auto-Fill This Shift**
3. Review AI recommendation with match score (e.g., "98% Match")
4. See why AI selected this candidate:
   - ✅ Required skills met
   - ✅ Available at requested time
   - ✅ AHPRA current and valid
   - ✅ Familiar with facility
   - ✅ Cost-effective option
5. Confirm or override

**For Multiple Shifts:**
1. Go to **Smart Match** page (purple highlight in sidebar)
2. View list of unfilled shifts (shows badge with number ready)
3. Click **Auto-Assign All** to let AI fill all shifts
4. Review recommendations in bulk
5. Confirm all or selectively approve
6. Assignments processed instantly

### Match Score Explained

- **90-100%** – Excellent match, highly recommended
- **75-89%** – Good match, suitable candidate
- **60-74%** – Fair match, acceptable with consideration
- **Below 60%** – Poor match, manual review needed

### Override Recommendations

You can always override AI suggestions:
1. View AI recommendation
2. Click **Change Staff**
3. Browse all available staff
4. Select preferred candidate
5. System explains difference in match score

### AI Learning

Smart Match improves over time by learning from:
- Your assignment decisions
- Staff performance ratings
- Shift completion outcomes
- Feedback and adjustments

## 9. Agency Management

Manage relationships with external staffing agencies and their contractors.

### Agencies (Providers)

**Provider Directory:**
- List of all contracted agencies
- Priority tier designation (Tier 1, 2, 3)
- Active contractor count
- Contract status
- Contact information
- Performance ratings

**View Provider Details:**
1. Click on any agency
2. See comprehensive profile:
   - **Agency Information** – Name, ABN, contact
   - **Contract Details** – Terms, rates, priority tier
   - **Contractor Pool** – All staff from this agency
   - **Shift History** – Recent placements
   - **Financial Summary** – Total spend, average rates
   - **Performance Metrics** – Fill rates, cancellation rates, staff ratings

**Add New Provider:**
1. Click **+ Add Provider**
2. Enter agency details:
   - Legal name and ABN
   - Primary contact information
   - Contract terms and rates
   - Priority tier designation
   - Insurance and compliance documents
3. Upload signed contract
4. Click **Create Provider**
5. Agency receives onboarding email

### Agency Staff (Contractors)

**Contractor Directory:**
All staff supplied by external agencies, including:
- Name and professional photo
- Supplying agency
- Role and qualifications
- AHPRA registration
- Availability status
- Rate information
- Performance ratings

**Key Differences from Hospital Staff:**
- Employed by agency, not Cabrini
- Premium rates apply
- Managed under provider contracts
- Limited direct management

**Contractor Profile:**

Similar to staff profiles but includes:
- **Supplying Agency** – Which provider employs them
- **Contract Rate** – Hourly or shift rate
- **Agency Contact** – Direct line to agency for issues
- **Cabrini History** – Previous placements at your facilities
- **Preferences** – Departments, shift types

**Add Contractor:**

Usually managed by agency, but you can:
1. Go to **Agency Management** → **Agency Staff**
2. Click **+ Add Agency Staff**
3. Select supplying agency
4. Enter contractor details
5. Upload credentials
6. Set rates and availability
7. Click **Create Contractor Profile**

### Priority Tiers

Agencies are assigned priority tiers affecting shift offers:

**Tier 1 (Preferred):**
- First offer on shifts
- Best rates negotiated
- Highest service standards
- Strong partnership relationship

**Tier 2 (Standard):**
- Standard offer sequence
- Market rates
- Reliable service

**Tier 3 (Backup):**
- Last option for coverage
- May have higher rates
- Used when other options exhausted

**Setting Priority:**
1. Open provider profile
2. Click **Edit Priority Tier**
3. Select tier (1, 2, or 3)
4. Add justification note
5. Save changes
6. Affects future shift offers

### Agency Performance Tracking

Monitor agency performance:
- **Fill Rate** – Percentage of offered shifts accepted
- **Cancellation Rate** – Late cancellations by their staff
- **Staff Ratings** – Average performance scores
- **Compliance Rate** – Credential maintenance
- **Response Time** – How quickly they respond to shift offers

### Contract Management

Track agency contracts:
- Contract start and end dates
- Rate cards and terms
- Renewal reminders
- Document repository
- Amendment history

## 11. Compliance & Credentialing

Centralized tracking of credentials, registrations, and compliance requirements.

### Compliance Dashboard

**Overview Metrics:**
- **Total Staff** – All active staff count
- **Compliant** – Staff with all credentials current (green)
- **Expiring Soon** – Credentials expiring within 30 days (orange)
- **Expired** – Non-compliant staff (red)
- **Compliance Rate** – Percentage compliant

### Required Credentials

NurseForce tracks:
- **AHPRA Registration** – Nursing and Midwifery Board registration
- **Working with Children Check** – Required for all clinical staff
- **National Police Check** – Background verification
- **Immunization Records** – Required vaccinations (COVID-19, Influenza, Hepatitis B, etc.)
- **Professional Indemnity Insurance** – Coverage verification
- **Annual Competency Assessments** – Skill verification
- **Specialized Certifications** – IV therapy, wound care, etc.
- **Manual Handling Training** – Workplace safety
- **Fire Safety Training** – Emergency procedures
- **Infection Control Training** – Basic requirements

### Compliance Status Tracking

**For Each Staff Member:**

View complete compliance profile:
1. Navigate to **Compliance & Credentialing**
2. Select staff member or filter by status
3. See all credentials with:
   - ✅ **Current** – Valid and up to date
   - ⚠️ **Expiring Soon** – Less than 30 days remaining
   - ❌ **Expired** – No longer valid
   - ⏳ **Pending Verification** – Document uploaded, awaiting review

### Expiry Alerts

**Automated Notifications:**

System sends alerts:
- **60 days before expiry** – Initial reminder to staff
- **30 days before expiry** – Second reminder with urgency
- **14 days before expiry** – Final warning, escalation to manager
- **On expiry date** – Staff automatically flagged as non-compliant
- **Managers notified** – Visibility of all expiring credentials

**Notification Settings:**
Configure who receives alerts for expiring credentials by department or staff type.

### Document Upload

**Staff Upload Credentials:**
1. Staff receive notification to upload
2. Log into NurseForce
3. Navigate to **My Profile** → **Credentials**
4. Click **Upload Document**
5. Select credential type
6. Upload file (PDF, JPG, PNG)
7. Enter expiry date
8. Submit for review

**Manager Verification:**
1. View pending credential uploads
2. Review document for authenticity
3. Verify expiry date
4. Approve or reject
5. System updates compliance status
6. Staff notified of approval

### Bulk Credential Management

**Export Expiring Credentials:**
1. Filter by "Expiring Soon"
2. Select date range (e.g., next 30 days)
3. Click **Export Report**
4. CSV/Excel download with:
   - Staff name and contact
   - Credential type
   - Expiry date
   - Days remaining
5. Use for targeted follow-up

### Compliance Reporting

**Generate Reports:**
- Compliance rate by department
- Expiring credentials forecast
- Non-compliant staff list
- Agency contractor compliance
- Credential type breakdown
- Historical trends

### Work Restrictions

**Auto-Enforcement:**
- Non-compliant staff automatically excluded from shift offers
- System blocks assignment to shifts
- Warning displayed if manual override attempted
- Audit log captures any overrides with justification

**Reinstatement:**
Once credentials updated and verified:
1. Compliance status automatically updates
2. Staff becomes eligible for shifts
3. Notification sent to staff and managers
4. Staff returned to available pool

## 15. Troubleshooting & Support

### Common Issues

#### Cannot Log In

**Problem:** Login fails or account locked

**Solutions:**
1. Verify correct email address
2. Check password (case-sensitive)
3. Try password reset:
   - Click **Forgot Password**
   - Enter email
   - Check inbox for reset link
   - Follow instructions
4. If still unable, contact your admin or support

#### Staff Member Not Appearing in Search

**Problem:** Can't find a staff member when assigning shifts

**Possible Causes:**
1. **Non-compliant** – Check compliance status
2. **Deactivated** – Staff account may be disabled
3. **Not available** – Staff marked as unavailable or on leave
4. **Insufficient skills** – Staff doesn't match shift requirements
5. **Wrong location** – Staff assigned to different location

**Solutions:**
- Use broader search terms
- Check compliance dashboard
- Verify staff profile is active
- Adjust shift requirements if too restrictive

#### Timesheet Won't Approve

**Problem:** Cannot approve submitted timesheet

**Possible Causes:**
1. **Missing information** – Required fields incomplete
2. **Rate mismatch** – Rate doesn't match contract
3. **Duplicate entry** – Shift already has approved timesheet
4. **Permission issue** – Your role may not have approval rights

**Solutions:**
- Review timesheet details for completeness
- Verify rate against agency contract
- Check for duplicate submissions
- Contact admin to verify your permissions

#### Agency Spend Shows Incorrect Amount

**Problem:** Budget dashboard shows wrong figures

**Solutions:**
1. Verify date range selected
2. Check location filter (single location vs. "All")
3. Ensure all timesheets are approved (pending timesheets not counted)
4. Refresh page to load latest data
5. If still incorrect, contact support with screenshot

#### Notifications Not Receiving

**Problem:** Not getting alerts for urgent shifts

**Solutions:**
1. Check notification settings:
   - Click **Settings** → **Notifications**
   - Ensure relevant notifications enabled
2. Check email spam/junk folder
3. Verify email address in profile is correct
4. Check browser notification permissions
5. Try logging out and back in

## Appendix

### Glossary

**AHPRA** – Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency; national regulatory body for health practitioners

**Agency Staff** – Healthcare workers employed by external staffing agencies and supplied to Cabrini

**Casual Staff** – Cabrini employees on casual contracts with flexible hours

**Compliance** – Meeting all credential, registration, and training requirements

**Contractor** – Same as Agency Staff; individual supplied by external provider

**Fill Rate** – Percentage of shifts successfully filled with qualified staff

**Hospital Staff** – Permanent Cabrini employees

**Priority Tier** – Ranking system for agency providers (Tier 1 = preferred)

**Provider** – External staffing agency supplying contractors

**Roster Cost** – Baseline cost of shift if filled with hospital staff

**Smart Match** – AI-powered feature for matching staff to shifts

**Supplemental Workforce Spend** – Additional cost of using agency staff above roster cost

**Timesheet** – Record of hours worked by staff member

**Workforce Pool** – All available staff (hospital, casual, and agency)
